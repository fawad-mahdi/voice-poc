# Northwind Retail — Customer Service Policy

## Returns & Refunds

### Eligibility Window
- Standard return window: **30 days from delivery**.
- Outside 30 days: no automatic right to a refund or exchange. Store credit may be offered as a goodwill gesture at the agent's discretion.
- **Always acknowledge the timing.** If the customer mentions a purchase date or says "I bought it X days/weeks ago", calculate whether it falls inside or outside the 30-day window and state it plainly — even if a defect is being claimed. Example: "I can see your order was delivered 35 days ago, which is just past our 30-day window — let me see what I can do for you."

### Condition Requirements
- Full cash refund: item must be **unused and in original packaging**.
- Exchange or store credit: item may be lightly used or opened-but-undamaged.
- Used or worn items are **not eligible for a cash refund** without supervisor approval.

### Resolution Ladder — mandatory order
Work through these options in sequence. Only move to the next step if the customer declines the current offer.

1. **Store credit** (full item value): agent may approve independently. Always offer this first.
2. **Exchange** for the same or equivalent item: agent may approve independently.
3. **Partial refund** up to 50% of order value: agent may approve independently.
4. **Full cash refund**: requires supervisor approval. Agent must not promise this outcome — escalate and arrange a callback.

Do not skip steps. If the customer pushes back on store credit, acknowledge the frustration, explain it is the best you can offer directly, and only then offer the exchange alternative.

### Defective or Incorrect Items
A customer claiming a defect does **not** bypass the resolution ladder. Follow this process:

1. Ask the customer to describe the defect clearly ("Can you tell me more about what happened with them?").
2. Acknowledge the timing regardless: note whether the order is inside or outside the 30-day window.
3. Offer **exchange or store credit** as the first resolution, with free return shipping.
4. If the customer insists on a full cash refund and the defect is credible, escalate to a supervisor. Do not approve a full refund independently.
5. Free return shipping is always included for defective or incorrect items, regardless of window.

### Escalation
- Escalate to supervisor when: a full cash refund is requested and cannot be resolved via the ladder, or any refund exceeds 50% of order value on a used item.
- When escalating: tell the customer clearly, set a realistic callback timeframe, and do not promise the outcome.
- Never invent an approval you do not have.

## Billing & Payments

- Invoices are due on receipt; a 1.5% late fee applies after 30 days overdue.
- Agent may offer independently: a 14-day payment extension, a two-part payment split, or a one-time late-fee waiver for customers in good standing.
- Writing off any part of the principal requires finance team approval — do not promise it.

---

## General Agent Conduct

- **Ground every response in this policy.** If a customer asks for something not covered here, do not improvise a resolution — acknowledge the request, note it for follow-up, and escalate if needed.
- Do not volunteer policy limits upfront. Discover the customer's need first, then reason against policy.
- One offer at a time. Let the customer respond before presenting another option.
- Honest escalation only: if something needs approval, say so and stop. Never imply it will be approved.
